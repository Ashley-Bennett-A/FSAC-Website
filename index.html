Welcome to Flintshire Sub-Aqua Club

Welcome

We are a BSAC Scuba Diving Club based in Connah’s Quay, North Wales. With over 50 years experience in providing beginners and qualified divers the skills to explore the fantastic coast of North Wales and Liverpool Bay, which contains a vast amount of sealife and wrecks of a wide range of shipping from submarines to passenger liners.
We have two RIB’s, as well as our own compressor, providing our own members with 232 bar or 300 bar fills.
We meet at the Connah’s Quay Leisure Centre every Wednesday at 7:30 where we provide theory lessons when needed, followed by practical lessons in the pool.

We meet socially at the Sir Gawain & The Green Knight, Golftyn Lane, Connah's Quay, Deeside CH5 4BH.

Curious?

If you are interested to learn more about scuba diving and experiencing our underwater world, then why not come along to our pool and do a Try Dive session?

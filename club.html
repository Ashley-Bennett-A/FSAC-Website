Our Club

This is a friendly and active British Sub Aqua Club based in Connah’s Quay, North Wales. We provide diver training from beginner through to Advanced Diver via the BSAC Diver Training Programme for all members . This includes a wide range of specialist training through the provision of regular Skill Development Courses.

The club has 2 advanced instructors, 10 open water instructors and two assistant instructors.

The club has an appointed welfare officer who, as a qualified social worker, has completed several safeguarding courses to ensure safety of all members is at the forefront of club activities.

What FSAC can offer:

  • Family Friendly
  • Active Social Calendar
  • Meeting/Training Rooms
  • Pool
  • Two RIB's (Rigid Inflatable Boat)
  • Snorkelling and try Dives offered to potential interested members
  • Scuba kit for learn to dive trainees to borrow
  • Active diving programme
  • Active BSAC Diver Training Programme
  • Teaches scuba diving from 14 years old
  • Own compressor
  • Club organised UK and overseas diving holidays
  • Snorkelling kit for learn to snorkel trainees to borrow
